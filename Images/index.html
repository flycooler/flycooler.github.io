Personal

I was born on Oct.14th 1980, Loudi, Hunan Province of China. In 1997, I enrolled in Zhejiang University. After four years' study, I received my B.S. degree from the Polymer Engineering Department and secondary B.S. degree from the Computer Science & Engineering Department in 2001. Then I pursued my M.S. at State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University. During my M.S. period, My research works was under the supervision of Prof. Qunsheng Peng and Associate Prof. Wei Chen. Currently, I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science in the Computer Graphics Group of the Max-Planck-Institut für Informatik in Saarbrucken, Germany. My supervisors now are Dr. Christian Theobalt and Prof. Dr. Hans-peter Seidel. In the same time, I feel extremely lucky to be able to cooperate with the genius researcher Prof. Jan Kautz, who is my idol all along.
