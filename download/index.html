Welcome to Zhao Dong's homepage












I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Program of Computer Graphics of Cornell University. I feel extremely lucky to be able to work with Prof. Donald Greenberg and my colleagues here. 

I was born on Oct.14th 1980, Loudi, Hunan Province of China. In 1997, I was enrolled in Zhejiang University. After four years' study, I received my B.S. degree from the Polymer Engineering Department and secondary B.S. degree from the Computer Science & Engineering Department in 2001. Then I pursued my M.S. at State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University. During my M.S. period, My research was under the supervision of Prof. Qunsheng Peng and Prof. Wei Chen. Since September 2005, I started to pursue my Ph.D. in the Computer Graphics Group of the Max-Planck-Institut für Informatik in Saarbrücken, Germany. My Ph.D.supervisors was Prof. Dr. Hans-Peter Seidel. During my Ph.D. time, I have been closely collaborating with Prof. Jan Kautz.  Also I had ever visited the Internet Graphics Group at MSRA under the supervision of Prof. Xinguo Liu and Computer Graphics Group at MSR under the supervision of Dr. John Snyder. My mentors are wonderful and I have learned so much from them for both my research and career!
