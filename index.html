Welcome to Zhao Dong's (董朝) homepage

I am currently a Senior Research Lead & Manager at Meta Reality Labs Research building the next-gen human-centric agent computing platform. Previously, I served as the Graphics Research Lead at the Technology Development Group of Apple, a Principal R&D Engineer with Autodesk's Cloud Rendering Service team, and a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Program of Computer Graphics of Cornell University.
In 1997, I was enrolled in Zhejiang University. After earning my bachelor's degrees, I started my research journey by pursuing my M.S. degree at the State Key Lab of CAD&CG of Zhejiang University. During this time, I had the privilege of working under the guidance of Prof. Qunsheng Peng and Prof. Wei Chen, exploring fascinating problems in graphics research. In September 2005, I embarked on my Ph.D. journey at the Computer Graphics Group of the Max-Planck-Institut für Informatik in Saarbrücken, Germany, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Hans-Peter Seidel. My Ph.D. research focused on GPU-based real-time rendering, where I had the opportunity to collaborate closely with Prof. Jan Kautz. Throughout my academic journey, I also visited the Internet Graphics Group at MSRA, working with Prof. Xinguo Liu and Computer Graphics Group at MSR, under the mentorship of Dr. John Snyder. These experiences, alongside the invaluable mentorship of my advisors, have profoundly shaped my research and career. I am deeply grateful for the knowledge and inspiration I have gained from them.
